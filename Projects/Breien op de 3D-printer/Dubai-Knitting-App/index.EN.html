Recipe Bracelet

An example: Knit 6 stitches (wide) and 40 pins (high).
You can disrupt the regular knitting pattern with the manipulation features.
Download the .gcode file and don't forget to send the .gcode file as an attachment to your email.
Pay the cost of your knitwear.
If you have received the print at home, take a glass or cup of hot water, let the print slowly soften in the hot water and push the print against the inner edge of your cup or glass do not force anything if it does not get soft, then your water is not hot enough. Allow the print to cool with cold water. If the print is cold, it stays in the right shape and your bracelet is ready!
