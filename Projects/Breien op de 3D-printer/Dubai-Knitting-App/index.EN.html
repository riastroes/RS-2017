you will get a flexible material with one layer, about 0.2 mm thick, it's not strong. With 5 layers the material will be stiff and a lot stronger. You can bend it in hot water, for ABS you need boiling water and for PLA water that's 60 - 70 degrees.

After generating a knitting you kan manipulate the result. The regular pattern of the knitting will be disordered by one of the manipulate functions. Play with it to see the effect. Recipe Bracelet

Make a knit with 8 stitches and 40 pins and 3 layers.
Put a number of points on the knit.
Manipulate the regular knitting pattern with a manipulate function.
Let the design print. Do not forget to send the .gcode file as an attachment to your email.
Pay the cost of your knitwear.
If you have received the print at home, take a glass or cup of hot water, let the print slowly soften in the hot water and shape it round inside the glass. Cool the print with cold water. If the print is cold, it stays in the right shape and your bracelet is ready.
